Use grep to find all lines containing the word "root" in /etc/passwd.

abhishek@Abhishek-OQG635QV:~/test_dir$ grep "root" /etc/passwd
root:x:0:0:root:/root:/bin/bash
abhishek@Abhishek-OQG635QV:~/test_dir$
